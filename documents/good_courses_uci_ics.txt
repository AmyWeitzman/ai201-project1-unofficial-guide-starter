I graduated with the Informatics B.S., HCI track in 2013. My favorite courses were: Social Analysis of Computerization (INF 161) - a good discourse on socio-technical issues of the day, also sometimes has an tech-unplugging assignment that is always interesting; Project in Medical Informatics (INF 172) was pretty interesting and interdisciplinary; Project in HCI & Evaluation (INF 132) was a good opportunity to learn about usability testing; and lastly the Senior Design Project (INF 191A-B-C) is the most useful, hands-on course I took.
