                                 Informatics

        RESTRICTIONS: Most IN4MATX courses will be initially restricted
        to majors in the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer
        Sciences ONLY. Some courses will be additionally restricted to
        specific majors or level(s) of standing. For restriction details
        and timeline: 
        http://ics.uci.edu/course-enrollment-restrictions/

        WAITLIST: Students on the waitlist for an ICS course should be
        in communication with that course's instructor to arrange for
        access to course materials and submission of any assignments
        while on the waitlist. Being on the waitlist or completing
        coursework DOES NOT guarantee a seat in that course.

        DISCUSSIONS & LABS: Discussion and lab sections begin meeting
        AFTER the first lecture. Always check for messages from your
        instructors before the start of instruction.

        Some lab sections are scheduled in laptop instructional labs ICS
        364A, ICS2 162, or ICS2 170. Students enrolled in these labs are
        required to bring a personal laptop to class meetings.

        B or X Restricted Courses: Courses with a B or X restriction
        require an authorization code from the instructor to enroll.
